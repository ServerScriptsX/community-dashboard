Frequently Asked Questions

Head Operators have the chance to become Shift Leader by working hard and meeting all of the requirements for a recommendation. The following document linked here goes through the requirements and stages involved with the Shift Leader recruitment process. Once you meet all of the requirements, you can be noticed by a Junior Corporate+ in the Human Resources Department by showing hard work, professionalism, and capability. All recruitment processes are done through our Discord server, so you must be in the server to participate.

High Ranks are recruited from our Board of Directors. To become a member of the Board of Directors, you must work hard through each rank of Shift Leader - General Manager. The Board of Directors is recruited by the Chief Operating Officers or Chief Human Resources Officers once spots are open to become a Junior Corporate of Human Resources or Operations. You will have to go through certain procedures and a trial period to become a permanent member of the High Rank team.
